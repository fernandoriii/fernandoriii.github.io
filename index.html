Fernando Reyes III

Fernando Reyes III was born on November 19, 2005, in Berwyn, Illinois, to Fernando Jesus II and Eduviges (Avila) Reyes.

Reyes is persuing a BS with a Major in Mathematics at the University of Illinois Chicago and is expected to graduate in the fall of 2025. He is a parishioner of Our Lady of Czestochowa and Charity Parish in Cicero, Illinois. He enjoys attending daily Mass, studying Sacred Scripture and Catechism, and computer programming.
